About Me

Irrigation systems sourcing from the fountainheads of streams in the Sierras have made the plains of North eastern and central California fertile lands for agriculture as never seen before. They are an engineering feat that supports the livelihood of many different crop farmers, which is why Ca irrigators can be seen as unified figures of great industrial change that is spearheading the shift into commercial farming practices.
