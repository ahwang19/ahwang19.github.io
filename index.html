About Me

Previously studying Commercial Dance at the California School of the Arts; San Gabriel Valley, Alison graduated in the Spring of 2019 to attend Occidental College. Her experience in the dance field includes certified training under the Royal Academy of Dance curriculum (Advanced 1), numerous performances from her home studio Ballet Kukan Academy, competitions in the Southern California area, group choreography, and youth dance education. Alison also has administrative and customer service experience from working as a dance instructor, office administrative assistant, and in the food service industry. Her strengths include efficient communication across a variety of ages and a welcoming personality with a strong work ethic.
